Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

1. Order Cancellation Overview

We understand plans change. You may cancel your order, but the cancellation conditions depend on the order status at the time of your request.

Quick Tip: Cancel as early as possible for the best outcome. Orders cancelled before processing receive full refunds.

2. Cancellation by Order Status

PENDING
Free Cancellation

Order not yet processed. Cancel anytime for a full refund within 24-48 hours.

CONFIRMED
Free Cancellation

Order confirmed but not yet prepared. Cancel within 2 hours of confirmation for full refund.

PROCESSING
Partial Refund

Order being prepared. Cancellation may result in a 10% processing fee deduction.

PACKED
Partial Refund

Order packed and ready. Cancellation incurs a 15% handling fee.

SHIPPED
Cannot Cancel

Order already dispatched. Please reject delivery or initiate a return after receiving.

DELIVERED
Return Process

Cancellation not possible. Please see our Return Policy.

3. How to Cancel Your Order

  1. Check your order status in "My Orders" section
  2. Contact us immediately via WhatsApp with your Order ID
  3. Request cancellation and provide reason (optional)
  4. Our team will confirm cancellation eligibility
  5. If approved, refund will be processed within 3-5 business days

Important: For fastest processing, contact us within 2 hours of placing your order.

4. Automatic Cancellation

AtFactoryPrice reserves the right to cancel orders in the following situations:

  • Payment Failure: Payment not received within 24 hours (for non-COD orders)
  • Out of Stock: Product becomes unavailable after ordering
  • Pricing Error: Obvious pricing mistakes on website
  • Verification Issues: Unable to verify payment or delivery details
  • Suspected Fraud: Unusual activity detected on account

In all automatic cancellation cases, you will receive a full refund if any payment was made.

5. Partial Order Cancellation

If your order contains multiple items:

  • You may cancel specific items before the order is shipped
  • Cancelled item amount will be refunded
  • Shipping charge adjustment may apply if minimum order threshold is affected

6. Custom Orders

For custom-made or specially ordered products:

No Cancellation: Custom orders cannot be cancelled once production has started. A 50% deposit is non-refundable if cancellation is requested before production.

7. Refund Processing

After successful cancellation:

  • Refund will be processed to original payment method
  • Processing takes 3-5 business days
  • Bank reflection may take additional 2-5 days
  • You will receive confirmation via WhatsApp/SMS

See our Refund Policy for detailed information.
